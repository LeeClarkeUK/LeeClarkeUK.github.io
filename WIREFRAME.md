3. Featured Projects

## Mobile Behavior
- Nav collapses to compact row with primary CTA.
- Hero stacks content first, then interactive module.
- Project cards become a single-column feed.
- Reduce decorative effects and particle density.

## Interaction Notes
- Hover on project cards reveals highlight edge.
- Scroll reveal is subtle and fast.
- Active nav link highlights based on scroll position.
